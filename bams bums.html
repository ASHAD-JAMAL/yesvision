What is BAMS Course?

Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery is a degree program which is for students who want to pursue and study modern medicine along with traditional Ayurveda. One of the most ancient systems in the world is Ayurveda, and it belongs to the Vedic period.
It’s an efficient system that helps to cure diseases as well as prevents them. This course helps the students understand the concepts of both modern medicine and Ayurvedic Medicine.

The system of Ayurveda is based on concepts like natural self-healing. It’s a system in which the body heals with the additional help and enhancement of Ayurvedic therapy. There are various advantages of Ayurvedic treatments and therapies.

It reduces the symptoms of the patient; it eliminates the impurities in the body of the patient and reduces the stress of the patient. Also, it helps in the prevention of further diseases in the body of the patient. The Ayurvedic treatments help the body heal, rejuvenate and prevent further diseases.

BAMS Course Structure

BAMS is a course in the medical field. Candidates will need to study both modern medicine and Ayurvedic Medicine for this course. The BAMS course duration is four years and six months of academic study and one year of practical learning. The first four years and six months will be academic sessions. Last year of the course the candidate must do an internship program with Practical training.

This course is divided into several professional courses. In the first year, the candidate will learn these professional courses- anatomy, physiology, and history of the ayurvedic system. The second-year professional courses are- toxicology and pharmacology. Lastly, the final course is surgery, ENT, skin and obstetrics, and gynecology.

The course offers both modern medicine knowledge as well as the knowledge of Ayurveda. The students who graduate from this course are known as “Vaidyar”.

About BUMS

BUMS is a 5-years undergraduate program that deals with studying the traditional healing and health maintenance system, the Perso-Arabic system of medicine based on the teachings of the Greek physicians Hippocrates and Galen. The job opportunities for the BUMS aspirants are available in both public and private sectors with job designations as Unani medical officer, hakims, pharmacist, research officer, assistant research officer.

"Bachelor in Unani Medicine and Surgery (B.U.M.S.) is a degree focused on Unani method of treatment offered in India, Nepal, Bangladesh, and other South Asian countries. Unani is a type of alternative treatment which includes the four-element earth, fire, water and air. The study in Unani treatment brings in more opportunities in the field of Unani Medicine." The BUMS full form is Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery. Students can find out the BUMS course details by visiting the college websites online.

Eligibility Criteria for BUMS

Admission for the BUMS course requires the aspirants to complete 10+2 with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in the examinations from a recognized school or board. In addition, it is mandatory to have science streams like physics, Chemistry, and Biology in class 12th. The age limit for BUMS is no less than 17 years old.
