About ANM Nursing

ANM Nursing is a two-year-long diploma course that deals with It primarily deals with the health care sector. The course provides knowledge about setting up operation theatre, taking care of various equipment, maintaining records,
and also administering timely medication to patients. Upon graduation, students can start roles such as Nurse, Assistant Nurse, Staff Nurse, Nursing Tutor, Healthcare Nurse, ICU Nurse etc.

Eligibility Criteria for ANM Nursing

For ANM Nursing course admission, the students need to know the eligibility criteria and all the ANM Nursing course details. The minimum educational requirements shall be the passing of senior secondary school certificate examination (10+2), 10th class or Central Board of Secondary Education or a recognized equivalent entrance examination with a minimum of 50% marks. There is no age limit to applying for this course.

Who Should Pursue an ANM Nursing?

ANM Nursing course is for those who want to work in the healthcare field and serve society with compassion, patience, and devotion. People who are motivated and hardworking, and who have a strong interest in medicine, should certainly apply for this course.

When To Do ANM Nursing?

The ANM Nursing course is a lesser-known course in India and the opportunities after completion of the course are very high. So, it is sensible to apply for the course right after completion of the 10+2 exam. Students from a science background who have scored a minimum of 50% in their 12th board exams are eligible to apply.

Types of ANM Nursing

Students can opt for this course in the full-time mode only as this course is a short course on nursing and requires hands-on experience on setting up operation theatres, handling medical equipment, cleaning and sanitization, etc. Following are the details of the ANM Nursing course:

Full-Time ANM Nursing

The two-year full-time ANM Nursing programme requires students to attend classes, submit work, and take examinations on campus. Students will receive a lot more exposure, experience, and understanding of the activities in medical facilities via direct connection with their peers, teachers, and doctors in a full-time ANM Nursing programme.

Part-Time ANM Nursing

ANM Nursing is a curriculum that is constantly evaluated and takes a practical approach. As a result, it is not available as a part-time educational programme.

Distance ANM Nursing

ANM Nursing is all about hands-on experiences in the medical domain. It takes a lot of concentration and hard work to be present on-site and actually handles the complex tasks in the hospitals as well as research centres. Owing to these reasons, distance ANM Nursing courses are not offered in India.
