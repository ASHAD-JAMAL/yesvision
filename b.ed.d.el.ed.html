About B.Ed

B.Ed full form is Bachelor of Education. The B.Ed course duration is two years, and the course focuses on teaching at a secondary level and higher secondary level division. According to Wikipedia, “A Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) is an undergraduate professional degree which prepares students for work as a teacher in schools. It may also be accompanied with or followed by tests for licenses or certifications required for teachers in some areas.”

B.Ed is a 2-year undergraduate course that deals with teaching at the secondary and higher secondary divisions. Formerly known as Bachelor of Training [BT] or ‘Siksha Shastri’, the B.Ed course opens up lots of job opportunities in the education field. Some popular B.Ed job roles include High School Teacher, Secondary School Teacher, and Assistant teacher, to name a few.

Eligibility Criteria for B.Ed

B.Ed admission is possible for the aspirants if the students ensure that they fulfil the B.Ed eligibility criteria. B.Ed course eligibility includes a minimum aggregate score of 50% in graduation is a requisite for admission. A minimum aggregate score of 55% at the postgraduate level is mandatory for admission into colleges for the B.Ed curriculum.
Some universities and colleges conduct entrance examinations for the selection of candidates. Most of the examinations are conducted in June or May. There is no B.Ed age limit for admission.

D.EL.Ed – Diploma in Elementary Education

D.EL.Ed stands for Diploma in Elementary Education. The course is a 2 year full time diploma course to train aspirants for teaching at primary level, that is classes 1 to 8.
D.EL.Ed admission 2022 is done at the state level and from this page, you can check details for all the states. Admission process differs from state to state. Candidates might have to take an entrance exam to qualify for this course in some state. However, some states take admission based on qualifying exam merit. The students who are 10+2 qualified and fulfill other eligibility criteria can apply for D.EL.Ed admission.

D.EL.Ed Course Eligibility

The eligibility to enroll in D.EL.Ed course is more or less the same for all the states. The general eligibility is given below. However, for specific eligibility, candidates must check the details for their respective states as they may vary.

Age limit-This may differ from state to state. But the lower age limit mostly ranges between 17 to 18 years. The upper age limit can range between 34 to 35 years.

Qualification- The qualification is 50% in 10+2 for general category students. Relaxation of marks is given to the reserved category candidates.
